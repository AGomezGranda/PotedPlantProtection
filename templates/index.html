About Us

We are an automted plant monitoring system based in Dundalk Currently Studying DkIT

How We Achieve This

With this project you can maximise the health and growth of your potted plants at home. Our target users range from regular people who have little to no technological experience or even experience with growing plants, to people who may have some experience in one of those areas and not the other. This system will cater to these types of users in several ways. This includes recommended growth conditions, simple IOT setup, and an understandable and accessible interface. Our goal is to assist and direct the system users on how to protect the plants they have at home and ensure they see to their maximum growing potential and lifespan.

This entails the use of sensors to record the plantsâ€™ health and condition. The sensors will record the crucial elements of the plants condition - water level, humidity, temperature, and light level. The application will allow for remote monitoring via an interface, of the plant conditions that have been recorded by the sensors. The system will also have summarized growing requirements for various plant types. This information will be in a connected database. With the help of our project a user can easily grow the perfect plant.

What Sensors will we use

  • DHT11 Heat & Humidity Sensor
  • Capacitive Soil Moisture Sensor
  • Si1145 Flora UV Index Sensor

Our goal is to provide each user with vital info such as "Heat", "Humidity", "Soil Moisture" and "UV index" and display it in a user-friendly way, so they would be able to see how their plant is doing whether they need to water or position the plant for more sunlight or heat/humidity

Our Services

Temperature Monitoring

We use the DHT11 as its power efficient to record temperatures every hour giving insight to the plants required heat conditions

Humidity Monitoring

We use the same DHT11 as used in heat sensor to record humidity every hour, allowing insight into the plants specific climate for growing

Sunlight Monitoring

We use the Si1145 to record the UV level every hour deciding if the plant receives enough sunlight or if it's too sunny to water plants

Soil Moisture Monitoring

We use a capacitive soil moisture sensor to negate corrosion allowing us to monitor the moisture of the soil indicating whether a user should water the plant or not

Notifications

From our sensors we will gather the information compare them against our data for specific plants and send you notifications based on urgency of the plant

Raspberry Pi 4

We use the Raspberry pi4 as a microcontroller to record data and send it onto a server so you are able to enjoy knowing the fact your plant is healthy
